Self-Care is More Than a Hot Bath & a Facial

Hi, I'm Rian River. I help busy women find work-life balance on a tight schedule.

About Me

Three years ago I was stuck in middle-management with a fridge full of spoiling Blue Apron meals and two weeks of unclaimed dry cleaning.

When that compulsory glass of wine after work stopped bringing me comfort, I started practicing gratitude and meditation.

Now I’m a holistic health coach for women who struggle to find balance in their everyday schedules.

How I Can Help You

Life Scheduling and Productivity Planning

Make time for the things that matter to you. Together we’ll map out your goals and create an easy-to-follow calendar that helps you finish what you’ve started with time to spare.

Personalized Self-Care Strategies

Self-Care means something different to everyone. I’ll help you discover what it means to you so you’re less likely to skip it and more likely to practice meaningful self-care methods on a regular basis.

Paleo Meal Prep Guides

Work-Life balance starts with a healthy diet. We’ll create a simple yet delicious Paleo meal plan that you can prep once a week so each meal is ready to go every day. No more dinner decision fatigue for you!
